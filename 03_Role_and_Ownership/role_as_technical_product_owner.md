# My Role as Technical Product Owner (TPO)

## Scope of Responsibility

As a Technical Product Owner, my responsibility went beyond
writing requirements or managing a backlog.

I was accountable for:
- Defining product scope and priorities
- Writing acceptance criteria with operational context
- Balancing delivery pressure with system risk
- Aligning QA, engineering, and stakeholders
- Owning product outcomes and consequences

## Dual Role Advantage (TPO + Tester)

Alongside TPO responsibilities, I remained closely involved in testing.

This enabled:
- Early visibility into real system behavior
- Better translation of test findings into product decisions
- Acceptance criteria aligned with real failure scenarios
- Reduced rework during integration and release

## Ownership Philosophy

Product ownership meant protecting users and the system,
even when it required difficult conversations or tradeoffs.
