# Challenges, Decisions & Key Learnings

## Challenges Encountered

- Delivery pressure conflicting with stability goals
- Late discovery of integration and recovery issues
- Limited visibility during long‑run execution
- Balancing stakeholder expectations with operational reality

## Key Decisions Made

- Deferred low‑value features to address system risk
- Strengthened release readiness criteria
- Prioritized clarity and predictability over speed

## What I Learned as a Product Owner

This project reinforced that:
- Stability is a product feature in industrial systems
- Observability is a user requirement
- Saying “not now” is a critical PO skill
- Early tradeoffs reduce long‑term pain

## Final Takeaway

Product ownership is about owning outcomes—not just shipping features.
