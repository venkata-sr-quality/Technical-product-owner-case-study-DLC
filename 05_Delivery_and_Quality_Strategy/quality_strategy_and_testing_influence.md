# Quality Strategy & Testing Influence

## Quality as a Product Responsibility

Quality was not treated as a downstream activity.

As a Product Owner, this meant:
- Treating stability and observability as backlog items
- Ensuring acceptance criteria were testable and meaningful
- Aligning testing focus with product risk, not checklist coverage

## How Testing Shaped Product Decisions

Being directly involved in testing revealed patterns such as:
- Failures appearing only after extended runtime
- Alarms lacking actionable context
- Recovery operations causing operator confusion

These insights directly influenced:
- Backlog reprioritization
- Acceptance criteria refinement
- Release readiness decisions

## Outcome

Testing became an **input into product strategy**,
not just validation at the end.
