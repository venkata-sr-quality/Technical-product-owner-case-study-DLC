# Product Vision, Goals & Success Criteria

## Product Vision

Deliver a DLC system that operators can rely on under:
- Normal operation
- Degraded conditions
- Failure and recovery scenarios

The system should behave **predictably**, not just functionally.

## Product Goals

- Ensure safe and stable system operation
- Surface meaningful and actionable alarms
- Enable confident recovery after faults or restarts
- Prevent unexpected behavior during long‑run execution

## How Success Was Measured

Success was not measured by feature delivery speed.

Instead, we looked at:
- Reduction in late‑stage and post‑integration issues
- Improved clarity during failure scenarios
- Increased confidence during long‑run testing
- Predictable release readiness

## Product Owner Mindset

In this domain, success means:
> Fewer surprises, even if it takes longer to deliver.
